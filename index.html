ONLINE ASSESSMENT PLATFORM

This is a full stack project that I built using React, Node.js, Express.js and MongoDB.
I developed an online exam platform where students can take assessments with mandatory screen sharing to ensure integrity. The platform saves videos of each exam, which can be reviewed by the admin.

Admins can create, update, and delete exams, while students can track their progress through interactive graphs. Both students and admins can compare performance across subjects and with other students.
